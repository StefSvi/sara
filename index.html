Bloom Mediation and Consulting (BMC) is dedicated to helping families, friends, and family businesses resolve their disputes in an efficient, productive manner. Is there a family that hasn’t been challenged by a falling out over children struggling with financial inequalities, siblings disagreeing on how to care for elderly parents, and multi-generational transfers of businesses and wealth?



Conflict is inevitable. What is not inevitable is having disputes that ruin families or valuable friendships. When complicated, emotionally charged conflicts arise in families or among close friends, there is not an obvious path to take toward resolution. Calling a lawyer begins an adversarial, expensive process. Few therapists are trained in conflict resolution, and the same is true of most clergy. Many people dread heated conversations, and feel these encounters are often unbalanced, leaving people more frustrated and angrier than they did before attempting to clear the air.


There are countless consultants dedicated to helping improve the functionality of a business. They are brought in to advise best practices, achieve specific goals, and offer guidance in turbulent times. But for most of us, our families and close friends are the key to our happiness. What happens when conflict interferes with a family’s ability to celebrate together, enjoy multi-generational gatherings, support each other, and in some cases work together?


Decades of research has shown that the quality of family bonds matter more to overall happiness than income levels. When these bonds are strong and peaceful, studies have shown that there is reduced stress and anxiety. People living in family conflict and prolonged disputes among close friends are at a greater risk for depression.


BMC offers a confidential, constructive way to navigate conflict, even when it feels the conflict is intractable.
